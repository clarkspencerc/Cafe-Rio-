Cafe Rio Recipe

This recipe has been a family favorite for a long time! We would always use it for special occasions, family reunions, grad parteis, and for a everyday dinner! It has brought smiles to many different generations as it has been shared and cooked with others! The true magic in making this recipe is that it is so simple and easy! I have never cooked as much food as I have when I use this recipe with such ease. You will feel like you are sitting in your very own Cafe Rio, on those colorful chairs enjoying the perfect meal in your own home, at the park, or where ever you may be! Just start following the directions already and get cooking!!

Prep Time: (insert time here) Cook Time (insert time here) Taste: Priceless

Sweet Pork

    You Will Need:
  • 1 Cup Salsa
  • 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  • 5 Pounds Sirloin Pork Roast

Add all ingredients to a crock-pot and cook until easily shredded (3-4 hours)

Zesty Chicken

    You Will Need:
  • 1 Small Zesty Italian Dressing (Kraft)
  • 1 Tbsp Chili Powder
  • 1 Tbsp Cumin
  • 3 Cloves of Garlic, Minced
  • 5 Pounds of Bonelss Chicken Breast

Put everything in a crock-pot for 4 hours, then shread the meat and roast for an additional hour. Also this is a TON of meat, you could do less than 5 lbs without any problems!

Tomatillo Salad Dressing

    You Will Need:
  • 1 Buttermilk Ranch Dressing Packet (make as recipe indicates)
  • 2 Tomatillos (tomato like veggie with a husk around them)
  • 1/2 Bunch of Cilantro
  • 1 Clove of Garlic
  • Juice of 1 Lime
  • 1 Jalapeño (include the seeds if you like it spicy! Can substitute jalapeño with a few drops of green Tobasco sauce

Use a food processor or blender to blend all of the ingredietns well. Refrigerate before serving.

Special Rice

    You Will Need:
  • 3 Cups Water
  • 4 tsp Chicken Bouillon
  • 4 tsp Garlic, Minced
  • 1/2 Bunch Cilantro
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin
  • 1 7oz Can Green Chilies
  • 3/4 tsp Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Butter
  • 1/2 Whole Onion
  • 3 Cups Rice

Blend cilantro, green chilies and onion in blender. Bring water to a boil and add all ingredients, steam for 30 minutes.
